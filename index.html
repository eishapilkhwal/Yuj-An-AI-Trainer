About the Project

Yuj | An AI-Trainer

Yuj is a next generation AI-Trainer, which will tell you, How to do Yoga.

Yuj is a Trainer who help you, see you and tell you, whether you are doing yoga pose correctly or not. And a Trainer who is available every-time. It predicts what pose you are making and How you are making it in real time. Basically, It predicts where the different parts of the body are present in image and use this data to predict the pose. If you are doing the pose correctly then teh timer will start and in case you did any mistake then the timer will restart.
